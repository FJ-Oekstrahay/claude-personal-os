Always express times as Eastern (ET/EDT/EST), not UTC.

**Why:** Geoff is in Eastern time. UTC timestamps are confusing and unhelpful to him.

**How to apply:** Any time you display or mention a time — logs, timestamps, Discord messages, cron schedules, etc. — convert to Eastern. Never display raw UTC.
