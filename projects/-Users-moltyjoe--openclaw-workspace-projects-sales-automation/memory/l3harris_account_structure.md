## Account Type & Compensation
- **GSS account** with 10% ownership/revenue share
- Compensated 10% on all L3Harris orders across all sites
- All L3Harris licensing consolidated into **single annual ELA** (enterprise license agreement)
- One renewal cycle per year (consolidates all sites' licenses)

- **Ft. Wayne, IN**

- **Massachusetts locations**
- **Virginia locations**
- **New Hampshire locations**

## Key People
- **Kim O'Grady** — GAM (Global Account Manager) for all of L3Harris

## Why Geography Matters
Single ELA at corporate level consolidates all sites' purchases. Revenue is centralized. Individual site engagement is important for relationship-building and technical direction, but purchasing/licensing is consolidated at corporate. Site managers have local technical influence but not separate P&Ls.
