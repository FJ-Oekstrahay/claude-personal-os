## Structural & Mechanical Simulation

- **Mechanical** — Structural simulation

## Fluid Dynamics & Thermal

- **Fluent** — CFD (computational fluid dynamics)
- **Thermal Desktop** — Thermal analysis and CFD

## Electromagnetic & Electronics

- **HFSS** — High-frequency electronics: antennas, signal integrity
- **HFSS Premium** — Advanced high-frequency (part of Electronics Premium tier)
- **Maxwell** — Low-frequency electromagnetics
- **Q3D** — 3D parametric electromagnetic field simulation (low-frequency)
- **Electronics Enterprise** — Bundle including Maxwell, HFSS, Q3D, and others
- **Electronics Premium** — Higher-tier electronics (includes Premium HFSS and other premium versions)

## Optics & Photonics

- **Zemax** — Optics/optical design
- **Speos** — Optics and light simulation
- **Lumerical** — Photonics/optics

## Digital Twin & AI/Optimization

- **Twin Builder** — Digital twin and AI
- **OptisLang** — Optimization and AI (also referred to as OptiSlang)

## Multi-Disciplinary & Systems Engineering

- **STK** — Satellites, air vehicles, space; digital mission engineering
- **ModelCenter** — MDAO (multi-disciplinary analysis and optimization), tool-chaining
  - **ModelCenter Enterprise** — Includes Behavioral Execution Engine (BEE), formerly Moxie
