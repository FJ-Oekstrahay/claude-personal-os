Never state a drone's manufacturer, model name, or specs from memory alone. Always read `hardware_inventory.md` first.

**Why:** Named Seeker3 as "Flywoo" when the inventory clearly says "DeepSpace". This kind of error is embarrassing in a product aimed at drone pilots who care about hardware specifics.

**How to apply:** Before any response that names a manufacturer or model (Seeker3, QAV, Tinyhawk, etc.) — even in passing — verify against `hardware_inventory.md`. The inventory is the source of truth. Do not infer manufacturer from prior conversation context.
