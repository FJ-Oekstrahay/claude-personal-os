Seymour web research run 2026-04-16. Full doc: `MARKET_RESEARCH.md`. Action items: `GEOFF_ACTION_REQUIRED.md`.

**Key numbers:**
- Hobbyist SaaS churn: ~6.2%/month (~71% annual) at sub-$25/mo
- Freemium conversion: 1–5%; 14-day trial with Day 3+7 check-ins hits 44.1%
- All FPV tuning/config software is free; only simulators are paid ($20 one-time)
- PIDToolbox is the only Patreon-gated tool — no public metrics

**Why:** The $10/mo subscription model is untested against a market that expects free tuning tools. Freemium cliff is the main risk — if free tier covers 80% of casual use, paid conversion is bottom-quartile.

**How to apply:** Don't revise pricing or free/paid feature split without acknowledging 6.2% monthly churn math. Flag that FPV pricing willingness-to-pay data is a gap, not an assumption.
